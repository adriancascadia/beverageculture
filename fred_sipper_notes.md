# Fred Sipper - "The Bottled Water King"

## Overview
Fred Sipper was dubbed "The King of Bottled Water" by Smithsonian Magazine for his revolutionary role in popularizing bottled water in the United States.

## Key Achievements

### Irving's Food Center (1960)
- Started selling Perrier in his small NYC grocery store in 1960
- Initially a ploy to attract European customers, especially French clientele
- First purchased cases, then pallets, then overseas containers from France
- Ran full-page advertisements in The New York Times

### Mootch and Muck Distribution (mid-1970s)
- Opened wholesale distribution company (named after parents' nicknames)
- First warehouse: 1,000 sq ft (mostly Perrier)
- Second warehouse (1982): 35,000 sq ft
- Third warehouse: 60,000 sq ft on Grand Avenue
- Fourth warehouse (1985): 100,000 sq ft in Williamsburg, Brooklyn

### Revolutionary Marketing Programs

#### The Evian Waiter Program
- Visited swankiest upscale restaurants, hotels, clubs in Manhattan
- Asked: "How much money do you make on free bread? What about free tap water you serve with ice that you pay for?"
- Showed restaurants that Evian at the table could add 15% to their checks
- Trained waiters: bottle of Evian on every table, no tap water glasses visible
- Waiters could earn extra $30 per night (30+ tables Ã— $1 per table)
- Created "Evian Mystery Shopper" - waiter received $100 on the spot if they mentioned Evian

#### Supermarket Innovation
- **Created the first bottled water sections in supermarkets**
- Convinced chain store buyers with risk-free offer: free fill for every inch of space
- Promised to buy back any unsold merchandise at full retail price at month's end
- Result: Never had to buy back any bottles
- Sold the concept to NYC's best retailers and supermarket chains

#### Bloomingdales Partnership
- Worked with Jack Maguire (CEO Evian USA, former VP Canada Dry)
- Convinced Marvin Taub at Bloomingdales to display Evian
- Sampled consumers in high-end departments (fur, expensive women's clothing)
- Secured 60-day exclusive for plastic Evian bottle introduction
- Pre-sold all city's supermarket chains leveraging Bloomingdales distribution

#### Food Emporium
- Worked with buyer Dan Portnoy
- Launched "Bottled Water Spectacular" - one week promotion four times per year

### NYC Marathon Sponsorship
- Partnered with Jack Maguire to sponsor NYC Marathon
- Catered to runners
- Both ran in Central Park at mid-day in pink Evian shirts and sweats
- Always carried glass Evian bottles (no plastic at that time)

### Current Work (2000-present)
- Consults for Beverageuniverse.com (second largest online non-alcoholic beverage distributor after Amazon)
- Still seeks new bottled waters daily
- Beverage Universe stocks 290+ kinds and sizes

## Impact
- Pioneered the concept of bottled water as a premium product
- Transformed bottled water from pharmacy medicine to lifestyle beverage
- Created distribution infrastructure that enabled industry growth
- Established marketing strategies still used today
